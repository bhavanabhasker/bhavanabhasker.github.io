About

I am a Data Scientist working for Comcast Labs at Sunnyvale. I have around 6 years of industry experience.I have previously worked as Data Science Research Assitant to my professor at San Jose State University; we built a Big Data pipeline for LAM Research.I also have work experience of 4 years as Data Analyst at SAP and Accenture.

Currently,I am working on learning word embedding for text data using deep learning architecture.I have built pipeline to automate text processing from ingestion phase to classification/recommendation phase.I also work on computer vision, object tracking and time series classification.
